Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how Fumicon collects and uses your information.

Introduction

At Fumicon, we are committed to protecting the personal information of our customers and visitors. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to keep it secure.

Information We Collect

  • Contact details: name, email address, phone number, and address when you request a quote or complete a booking.
  • Service details: package selection, preferred service date, and any service-specific requirements.
  • Feedback: messages and ratings submitted via our feedback forms.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and requests.
  • To schedule and deliver our fumigation and sanitization services.
  • To improve our website, services, and customer support.
  • To send you updates only if you opt in to receive them.

Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with trusted service providers who support our operations, such as booking confirmation or payment processing. We use appropriate security practices to protect against unauthorized access.

Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies or similar technologies to improve your browsing experience and analyze site usage. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to review, update, or request deletion of your personal information.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@fumicon.com.

This Privacy Policy is effective as of June 2026 and may be updated as needed.
